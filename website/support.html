Support

Last updated: March 14, 2026

For support requests, pairing issues, notification-delivery problems, or privacy-related requests, contact SupaPhone at:

altusbranding@gmail.com

Before you contact support

  • Open the Android app once and confirm the paired device still appears in the extension.
  • Use the refresh button in the extension popup to sync the latest paired-device state.
  • Check that Android notifications are allowed for SupaPhone on the target device.
  • Confirm the browser extension and Android app are both updated to the current version you are testing.

Helpful details to include

  • Your browser name and version.
  • Your Android device model and Android version.
  • Whether the problem is in the extension, Android app, or both.
  • Any error text shown in the popup or app.
  • Whether the issue affects phone-number sends, link sends, or both.

Common support topics

  • Pairing issues with the QR flow or 6-digit code flow.
  • Notification delivery delays or missing pushes on Android.
  • Chooser actions for Call, Open in dialer, WhatsApp, Share, Open link, Copy link, or Share link.
  • Browser extension quick-send, device refresh, or paired-device status issues.
  • Ad privacy choices, consent flow, banner behavior, or app-open ad behavior in the Android app.

Support scope

SupaPhone support covers the SupaPhone browser extension, Android app, and hosted delivery flow. Support does not cover carrier issues, WhatsApp account restrictions, or device-manufacturer battery settings outside the app's control, though those details can still help diagnose a problem.
