# **SupaPhone \- Application Overview Document**

## **1\. Introduction**

**Application Name:** SupaPhone

**Core Concept:** A productivity bridge connecting your desktop browser and Android device. SupaPhone allows you to push phone calls and web links from your computer to your phone via a simple right-click, eliminating manual typing and searching.

**App Name**: SupaPhone: Browser to Mobile Link

---

## **2\. Setup & Pairing**

To sync the **Browser Extension** and the **Android App**:

### **Pairing Process**

To link your browser to your phone, SupaPhone uses a secure, instant pairing system:

* **Method A (current default):** Enter a unique **8-digit code** generated by the extension into the app.
* **Method B:** Scan the pairing QR shown in the extension.

### **Connection Model: Many-to-Many**

SupaPhone is built for power users. You aren't limited to just one setup:

* **Multiple Browsers to One Phone:** Connect your work laptop and home desktop to the same device.
* **One Browser to Multiple Phones:** Send links or calls to your personal phone, work phone, or a tablet simultaneously.

---

## **3\. Feature 1: Click-to-Call**

* **Execution:** Right-click any phone number on a webpage.
* **Direct Access:** Your paired **Device Name(s)** appear directly in the context menu. Clicking the name sends the notification instantly.
* **Mobile Notification Options:**
  * **Call:** Executes the call path (permission-aware).
  * **WhatsApp:** Opens WhatsApp/WhatsApp Business/compatible installed variant.
  * **Default:** Tapping the notification body opens **Dialer** with number pre-filled.
* **Regional Number Handling:** Android app applies region-aware normalization so local numbers can route correctly for WhatsApp.

---

## **4\. Feature: Link Sharing**

Instantly move your browsing session from your desktop to your mobile device.

* **The Trigger:** Right-click on any **hyperlink** or right-click the **page background** to share the current URL.
* **Device Selection:** Select your device name directly from the right-click context menu.
* **Mobile Interaction:** A notification appears on the Android device showing the page title or URL with two clear options:
  1. **Copy Link:** Copies the URL to your phone's clipboard for easy pasting into other apps (WhatsApp, Notes, etc.).
* **Default Behavior (current):** If you tap the main notification area (instead of a specific button), it defaults to **Copy Link**.

---

## **5\. Technical Specifications & App Settings**

### **Architecture**

* **Backend:** **Supabase** (Handles the real-time data sync and push triggers).
* **Frontend:** Android App \+ Browser Extension (Manifest v3).

### **Key Settings**

* **Battery Optimization:** A dedicated setup guide within the app to ensure the phone doesn't "sleep" on incoming notifications.
* **Theme Engine:** Toggle between **Dark** and **Light** modes.
* **Add Device:** Pair additional browsers directly from Android Home without unpairing the phone.
* **WhatsApp Region Selector:** Choose region on Android Home so local numbers normalize correctly for WhatsApp actions.
* **Device Identity:** Rename your devices (e.g., "Personal Pixel" or "Work Samsung") so they are easy to distinguish in the browser menu.
